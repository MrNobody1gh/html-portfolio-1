My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Fast And Furious 9

A visually stunning and intellectually complex film, Inception dives into the world of shared dreaming and subconscious manipulation. With Leonardo DiCaprio leading a talented ensemble cast, the film challenges viewers to question reality itself. Nolan’s direction, the layered storytelling, and Hans Zimmer’s pulsating score combine to create a thrilling cinematic experience

Parasite 2019

A masterclass in storytelling and social commentary, Parasite explores class inequality through the lens of a poor family that slowly infiltrates the lives of a wealthy household. With shocking twists and a unique blend of humor and horror, the film is both entertaining and deeply thought-provoking.
It became the first non-English film to win the Best Picture Oscar, and rightfully so.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A timeless story of hope, friendship, and perseverance, The Shawshank Redemption follows a wrongly convicted man’s life inside prison and his journey toward redemption. The performances by Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman are deeply moving, and the film's pacing, narration, and emotional payoff make it a must-watch. It’s not just a prison drama it’s a lesson in the human spirit.
